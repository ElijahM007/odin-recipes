Cosmic Brownies

Description

Little Debbie has some competition!

Ingredients

Brownie

  • Baking spray
  • 14 ounces bittersweet chocolate bar
  • 1 1/2 cups unsalted butter
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 6 tablespoons canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 2 1/3 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Ganache

  • 7 ounces semisweet chocolate bar; chopped
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1/8 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup rainbow candy chocolate pieces

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  3. Microwave chocolate and butter until fully melted and smooth. Whisk in cocoa, canola oil, and vanilla until fully combined.
  4. Whisk sugar and brown sugar into chocolate mixture until combined. Add eggs, flour, cornstarch, salt, and baking powder until just combined.
  5. Transfer to prepared baking pan.
  6. Bake until set. Remove from oven and let sit at room temperature for about 1 hour. Place in refrigerator to chill completely.
  7. Prepare Ganache: Place chocolate in a medium mixing bowl. Bring heavy cream to a simmer in a small saucepan. Pour heavy cream over chopped chocolate. Whisk in butter and salt.
  8. Remove brownies from pan and spread ganache on top. Sprinkle evenly with rainbow candies and chill until ganache is set.
  9. Cut brownies and serve!
